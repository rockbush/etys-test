## What To Check After Posting

Open Google Analytics and watch:

- `page_view`
- `calculator_viewed`
- `calculator_used`
- `listing_viewed`
- `listing_generated`
- `waitlist_viewed`
- `waitlist_submit_clicked`
- `waitlist_submitted`

Good early signs:

- 20-50 visits from the first posts.
- `listing_generated` keeps happening.
- `calculator_viewed` is much higher than `calculator_used`, which means calculator copy or inputs need improvement.
- Any `waitlist_submitted` event from a cold visitor is a strong signal.
