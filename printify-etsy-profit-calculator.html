Why POD Profit Needs Its Own Check

Print-on-demand sellers often work with lower margins because production and shipping costs are fixed by the supplier. A product can look profitable at the retail price but become weak after Etsy fees, payment processing, ads, discounts, and sample costs.

Costs To Include For Printify Products

  • Printify production cost for the selected product and variant
  • Supplier shipping cost and any shipping charged to the buyer
  • Etsy listing fee, transaction fee, and payment processing estimate
  • Design, mockup, sample, and ad costs
  • Discounts, returns, and replacement assumptions
  • Offsite ads if they apply to the order

How To Use This For POD Product Research

Pick one product idea, add the Printify cost and shipping cost, then test several selling prices. If the margin only works at a price buyers are unlikely to pay, the product may need a better niche, better design, or lower-cost production.
