Use of the Tool

SellerMargin is a static MVP that helps Etsy sellers estimate product profit and draft listing copy. You may use the calculator and demo generator for planning, research, and product validation.

Estimates and Accuracy

Fee, profit, margin, and break-even calculations are estimates. Etsy fees, payment processing fees, taxes, shipping rates, ads, discounts, and seller circumstances can change. Confirm final numbers in your Etsy dashboard and accounting records before making business decisions.

Generated Content

Listing titles, tags, descriptions, and SEO suggestions are draft outputs. Review generated content for accuracy, originality, marketplace compliance, and fit with your actual product before publishing.

No Professional Advice

SellerMargin does not provide legal, tax, financial, or accounting advice. The tool is provided for informational use by sellers who remain responsible for their pricing, compliance, and business decisions.

Future Paid Features

Pricing, credits, subscriptions, and AI generation limits shown in this MVP are draft concepts and may change before public launch.
