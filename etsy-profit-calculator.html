What This Etsy Profit Calculator Includes

SellerMargin helps Etsy sellers estimate real product profit instead of only looking at the selling price. The calculator is designed for handmade sellers, digital product sellers, custom product shops, and print-on-demand sellers who need a faster way to test pricing before listing.

  • Product selling price and shipping charged to the buyer
  • Product cost, shipping cost, and packaging cost
  • Etsy transaction fee and listing fee estimates
  • Payment processing fee estimate
  • Optional Etsy ads and offsite ads
  • Net profit, profit margin, break-even price, and suggested target price

Why Etsy Sellers Need Profit Math Before Listing

A product that looks profitable at first can become thin-margin after shipping, packaging, discounts, ads, and platform fees. Checking the margin before listing helps you decide whether to raise the price, reduce costs, change shipping, or skip the product.

How To Use The Calculator

  1. Enter the product price you plan to charge.
  2. Add product cost, shipping cost, and packaging cost.
  3. Add ad spend or offsite ads if they apply.
  4. Change any number and watch the margin update instantly.
  5. Use the break-even price and target price as pricing guardrails.

Common Etsy Costs To Check

Sellers often remember material cost but forget packaging, samples, returns, discount codes, payment processing, offsite ads, and the cost of acquiring a buyer. The calculator gives you a single place to test those assumptions.
