Which Etsy Fees Should You Include?

Etsy sellers should estimate more than one fee. A useful pricing check includes the listing fee, transaction fee, payment processing, shipping cost, packaging, discounts, ads, and any other cost required to deliver the order.

  • Listing fee for publishing or renewing a listing
  • Transaction fee applied to order revenue
  • Payment processing fee estimate
  • Optional offsite ads fee when applicable
  • Shipping and packaging costs paid by the seller
  • Discounts, coupons, and ad spend

Fee Estimates Are Pricing Inputs

Fee calculators are most useful when combined with product cost and shipping cost. Looking at fees alone can be misleading because a low-fee product can still have a weak margin if production or shipping cost is high.

How SellerMargin Uses Fee Estimates

The live calculator combines estimated Etsy fees with your product costs and returns net profit, profit margin, break-even price, and a suggested target price. That makes it easier to compare multiple products before listing.
